Enterprise App Solutions

Working With Enterprise Clients

Whilst I am not able to directly discuss the details of individual client projects, I can say that working on enterprise products whilst at Veam Studios has given me valuable experience designing for a wide variety of specifications. For some products, analytic dashboards were created, some required capturing over 300 different data fields in one screen. Client requirements were not always clearly defined, so asking questions and listening to the clients response was a very important skill to perfect early on. Iterating on several feedback cycles, as well as collaborating with different company departments also taught me the importance of an inclusive design process. This process brought together the expertise of many people and which I was able to process and refine into a concise, well designed user interface.

Working with a variety of clients has allowed me to observe the many different ways people work, and taught me the importance of observation, listening and understanding.
